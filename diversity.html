How Broad Was Each Post?

Some posts spread their energy across many themes (high diversity). Others concentrate on just one or two (low diversity). Shannon entropy captures this in a single number — a measure of how evenly a post's thematic energy is distributed.

Each dot is a post. The red line shows the moving average. Higher means more thematically diverse.
