The Seven Themes

Each post belongs to one of seven categories assigned during writing. Here's how the corpus distributes across them.

Teaching Strategies and AI & Technology together account for more than half the corpus, reflecting the blog's dual focus on pedagogical practice and technological change.

What Did LuddyTeach Talk About?

Beyond the seven editorial categories, every post was scored against 15 cross-cutting keyword dictionaries. These dictionaries capture concepts that cut across categories — a Teaching Strategies post might score high on scaffolding, metacognition, and active learning all at once. The dictionaries fall into four conceptual clusters.

Technology Tools leads by a wide margin, reflecting the blog's roots in a computing education department. Assessment Design and Equity & Inclusion follow, anchoring the blog's dual commitment to rigorous evaluation and inclusive practice. Metacognition and Active Learning trail — not because they're absent, but because their vocabulary is more diffuse.
